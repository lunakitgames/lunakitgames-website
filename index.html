The Throne of The Forgotten Spell

2.5D Fantasy Platformer • 1–4 Player Co-op • 10 Kingdoms

A young Dwarf Prince is kidnapped during his crowning ceremony by a mysterious Witch who seeks an ancient spell that protects the most powerful kingdom in the realm. Now, Elf warriors must journey across 10 breathtaking kingdoms — from enchanted forests to haunted castles, frozen tundras to floating cloud islands — to rescue the Prince and uncover a dark secret that will change everything they thought they knew about their enemy.

With deep character customization, a wise Gnome companion riding on your shoulder, five weapon types including the legendary Magical Orb Bomb, and a story that hides its greatest twist in plain sight — this is a fantasy adventure designed from the heart.

Our Story

About Luna Kit Games

Luna Kit Games started with a simple idea: what if an eight-going-on-nine-year-old could design a real video game?

Addy has always been a creator — drawing characters, inventing worlds, and telling stories that surprise everyone around her. When she started sketching Elves, Dwarves, and magical kingdoms, her dad saw something special. Not just a kid doodling, but a genuine game designer with a vision.

So they made a deal: Addy would be the Creative Director and Game Designer — designing the characters, the story, the worlds, and the art. Addy's Daddy would handle the technical side — setting up the tools, managing the code, and making sure her vision could actually be played.

Together, armed with AI tools, a game engine, and a LOT of imagination, they're proving that the next great game studio doesn't need a big office or a huge team. It just needs a great story, a bold creative vision, and the determination to build it.

Luna Kit Games is named after Addy's love of foxes (a kit is a baby fox) and the magic of moonlight (luna). The studio logo — a sleeping fox kit curled around a crescent moon — represents the dreams that become reality when you're brave enough to build them.

Addy
Creative Director & Game Designer
The eight-going-on-nine-year-old visionary behind The Throne of The Forgotten Spell. Addy designs every character, paints the original concept art, writes the storylines, names the kingdoms, and makes every creative decision. Her hand-drawn mandala became the stained glass window in the Dwarf Castle. This is her game.
When she's not designing video games, Addy loves storytelling, crafting, playing sports, sailing, and spending time with her pets. She has three cats, two dogs, two guinea pigs, one hamster, and a fish — so her world is never short on characters.
Addy's Daddy
Technical Direction & Development
Mechanical engineer turned vibe coder and tech entrepreneur. He handles the technical infrastructure — Unity, GitHub, deployment, and AI-assisted development. His job is to make sure Addy's creative vision becomes a playable reality.
Outside of Luna Kit Games, he enjoys spending time creating and building with his two kids, hanging out with the family's many pets, and adventuring with his wonderful fiancée. A tech entrepreneur for the last 15 years, he fills his free time with vibe coding, building businesses, flying planes, riding motorcycles, spending time in nature, and exploring the world.
